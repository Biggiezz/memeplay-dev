üéØ What is a Bonding Curve?

A bonding curve is a mathematical smart contract that automatically prices tokens based on supply bought. The more people buy, the higher the price‚Äîno manual market makers needed!

üöÄ 3-Phase Token Lifecycle:

Phase 1: Token Creation (1 Minute)

When creating a game, you fill 3 simple fields:

  • Token Name: "Cat Jump Coin"
  • Symbol: $CATJUMP (3-10 characters)
  • Supply: 1,000,000,000 (recommended) or custom

That's it! No allocation, no vesting, no complexity. Token deploys in 1 click.

Phase 2: Bonding Curve Trading ($0 ‚Üí $69K)

How it works:

  • Starting Price: $0.000001 (Market Cap = $0)
  • Anyone Buys: Price increases along mathematical curve
  • Anyone Sells: Price decreases, funds returned from curve
  • Creator Gets: Nothing upfront (fair launch!)

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need crypto knowledge to play?

No! You can play all games without a wallet. Just visit memeplay.dev and start playing. To earn PLAY rewards or donate, you'll need to connect a wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.), but it's optional.

Can I edit my game after publishing?

Yes! Use the "Edit Game" button on your creator dashboard. Changes are live instantly. However, you cannot change the token (symbol, supply) after launch‚Äîonly game code/graphics.

What happens if my game gets no plays?

No penalties! Games with 0 plays simply don't appear on homepage. You can delete and republish with a better concept. No financial loss (except small gas fees).

Is the bonding curve safe? Can I lose money?

Yes, it's safe from rugs, but crypto is risky! Bonding curves eliminate rug pulls (no team tokens, auto-locked liquidity), but token price can still go down if people sell. Only invest what you can afford to lose!

How do I cash out my earnings?

Three ways:
1. Donation earnings (BNB/USDT): Withdraw instantly to your wallet via dashboard
2. PLAY rewards: Swap on PancakeSwap for BNB/USDT (coming soon)
3. Game token: Sell on bonding curve (pre-graduation) or PancakeSwap (post-graduation)

Can I delete my game and token?

Game: Yes. You can hide/delete from MemePlay anytime.
Token: No. Once deployed on blockchain, tokens are permanent (decentralized!). However, if it never graduates ($69K MC), it stays on bonding curve and fades into obscurity.
